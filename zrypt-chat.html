2. How We Use Your Data:
We use this data solely to provide the chat service, troubleshoot bugs, and maintain the security of our platform.

Because we do not collect emails, account recovery or verification is currently not possible. If you wish to delete your account, please contact us at xueltimiogok@gmail.com with proof of account ownership.
